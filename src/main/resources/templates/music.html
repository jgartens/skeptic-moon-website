Listen To Skeptic Moon

Days before our bassist and friend Sam Kohler left to attend Peabody in Baltimore, Skeptic Moon went into Funky Nola Studio on North Broad Street and recorded a 7-track album. Two days in the studio, with the help of Johnny and Todd, wielded our first studio album, which is currently in the processes of being mixed by our keyboardist, Jamie Koffler.
