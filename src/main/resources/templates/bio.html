Skeptic Moon is a band formed in New Orleans, Louisiana in March 2019. Skeptic Moon is quickly carving a unique niche on the New Orleans scene. A growing repertoire of original music, written collaboratively, indicates a distinct approach to music-making. The band's style draws from each member's mutual love of jazz, fusion, progressive rock, classical, experimental noise, metal, and hip-hop styles, leading to a trademark sound.

Jake plays the drums. He hails from Brattleboro, Vermont. He draws inspiration from his favorite bands and artists, which currently include King Crimson, Christian Scott ATunde Adjuah, My Morningn Jacket, and IK Diaro.
