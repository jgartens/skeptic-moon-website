Skeptic Moon is a band formed in New Orleans, Louisiana in March 2019, and is quickly carving a unique niche in the music scene. A growing repertoire of original music, written collaboratively, indicates a distinct approach to music-making. The band's style draws from each member's mutual love of jazz, fusion, progressive rock, classical, experimental noise, metal, and hip-hop styles, leading to a trademark sound.

Jamie Koffler was born and raised in New Orleans, LA. He holds down keys, the pItCH wHeEl, noises and so much more. Jamie is currently in the process of mixing Skeptic Moon's first album. He listens to Chick Corea, mosaic.wav, Kanye, Black Uhuru Slayer, Thundercat, Takako, and Minekawa.
Next up is Michael SzE, violinst extrordinarie from San Fransisco. Michael is fucking nuts, just wait for the album. His inspirations include Jean Luc Ponty, John Coltrane, Charlie Parker, Jascha Heifetz, Anomalie, and Bad Snacks.
On tenor saxophone and flute is Ryan Park, who comes from Newton, MA. Some have called his saxophone playing "THe only palatable part of the group!" Right now, Ryan's into Anderson.Paak, The Bad Plus, Paul Desmond, Sam COoke, Cecil McLoran Salvant, Dexter Gordan, and Joshua Redman.
Jake Gartenstein plays the drums. He hails from Brattleboro, Vermont -- the Greeen Mountain State. He draws inspiration from his favorite bands and artists, which currently include King Crimson, Christian Scott ATunde Adjuah, My Morningn Jacket, and IK Diaro.
